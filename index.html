This is the 2D Jump & Run story of Pepe and his chicken farm. The chickens have been infected by a virus and have been attacking Pepe and all his neighbours ever since. Help Pepe to repel the invasion of the chickens. The little chickens can only be defeated with the help of his jumping power from above. The big final boss chicken must be defeated using the salsa bottles. But be careful, there are not an infinite number available!

Move Left: A
